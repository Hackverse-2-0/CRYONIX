# Team Selector Fix - User Guide

## ✅ What Was Fixed

1. **Team Selector Now in Top Bar**
   - The team selector dropdown is now visible in the top navigation bar (right side)
   - Appears on all pages (Dashboard, Tasks, Notes, Analytics)

2. **Improved Dropdown Styling**
   - Dark background for dropdown options (easier to see)
   - Proper hover states
   - Native browser select appearance restored

3. **Better Create Team Flow**
   - Modern glassmorphism modal
   - Clear error messages if something goes wrong
   - Page refreshes after successful team creation

## 🎯 How to Use

### Creating Your First Team

1. **Find the Team Selector**
   - Look in the TOP RIGHT corner of the page
   - You'll see a blue gradient button: '+ New Team'

2. **Click '+ New Team'**
   - A glassmorphism modal will appear

3. **Fill in Team Details**
   - Team Name: e.g., 'Alpha Squad'
   - Project Name: e.g., 'Mission Phoenix'

4. **Click 'Deploy Team'**
   - The team will be created
   - You'll see it in the dropdown immediately
   - The page will refresh to load your new team

### Switching Between Teams

1. **Find the Dropdown**
   - Located in the top bar (next to '+ New Team' button)
   - Shows 'Select Team' if no team is selected

2. **Click the Dropdown**
   - You'll see all your teams listed
   - Options have dark background for visibility

3. **Select a Team**
   - Click on any team name
   - The dashboard will update immediately

## 🔍 Troubleshooting

**Issue: Dropdown is empty**
- Make sure you've created at least one team
- Check that you're logged in
- Try refreshing the page

**Issue: Can't see dropdown options**
- The options now have a dark background (rgb(15 23 42))
- Make sure you're clicking directly on the dropdown

**Issue: Team not showing after creation**
- The page should auto-refresh
- If not, manually refresh the browser (F5 or Ctrl+R)
